## IMPORTANT NOTES ON URL STATUS

Three URL types exist in this reference:
- **VERIFIED DIRECT** - full https:// URL confirmed to lead directly to the document
- **VERIFIED PORTAL** - URL confirmed to lead to the correct portal page; navigation instructions provided
- **UNVERIFIED** - URL provided by research but not independently confirmed; flag before citing
- **UNAVAILABLE** - no public URL exists; document accessed via institutional subscription or archive only
