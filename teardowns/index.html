About this series

Each teardown follows the same analytical pass: identify the buyer when they differ from the user, read the GTM motion for what it signals about positioning, name one specific product-level friction or strength without guessing at what cannot be verified, and propose one concrete recommendation. It is the same operator discipline behind the rest of Growth Operator Notes, applied one product at a time.
