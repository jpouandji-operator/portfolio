# GON REGULATORY DOCUMENT INVENTORY
**Created:** March 31, 2026
**Owner:** Jean-Philippe Ouandji - Growth Operator Notes
**Purpose:** Master checklist of all regulatory, legal, and compliance documents required to support GON articles, IC Checklist dimensions, and war games retrospectives. Organised by market and sector. Status tracked against three acquisition states: NAMED (instrument identified), SOURCED (URL and key provisions confirmed), SUMMARISED (operator summary written and added to repository).

---

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

- Every instrument listed here must eventually reach SUMMARISED status before the regulatory library goes public
- NAMED instruments are placeholders - they exist but we have not yet confirmed the document
- SOURCED instruments have a confirmed URL and key provisions in the benchmark repository
- SUMMARISED instruments have an operator-plain-English summary written alongside the technical provisions
- Use the weekly research prompts to move instruments from NAMED to SOURCED
- Priority column reflects IC Checklist relevance - HIGH means it directly affects a current tool dimension
